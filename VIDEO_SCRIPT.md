# AI HR Employee — Video Script

**Duration:** ~4 minutes
**Tone:** Professional, walkthrough-style, screen recording with voiceover

---

## 0. Opening (0:00 – 0:15)

**Visual:** Login screen with 3 role buttons

**Voiceover:**
"Welcome to AI HR Employee — an AI-powered HR platform built with Next.js, Prisma, and Google Gemini. Let's take a quick tour."

---

## 1. Login & Dashboard (0:15 – 1:00)

**Visual:** Click HR role → Sign In → Dashboard with stat cards and charts

**Voiceover:**
"Three quick-login roles auto-fill credentials. As HR, the dashboard shows key metrics — total employees, pending leaves, active recruitments — plus trend charts, department distribution, and pending approvals. The sidebar is role-aware: HR sees everything, Managers see their team, Employees see only their own data."

---

## 2. Recruitment to Onboarding (1:00 – 2:00)

**Visual:** Recruitment page → evaluation form → onboarding workflow

**Voiceover:**
"HR creates job openings and manages candidates. Each candidate gets an AI screening score via Gemini. After interviews, evaluators score candidates on four parameters, and AI generates a summary with recommendation — Strong Hire, Hire, Maybe, or No Hire. Once hired, the 10-step Onboarding workflow runs automatically: creating the profile, employee ID, company email, assigning training, and generating a welcome letter — all in real-time."

---

## 3. AI Workflow Engine & Key Modules (2:00 – 3:00)

**Visual:** Workflow page typing natural language → Leave page apply/approve → Performance reviews

**Voiceover:**
"The AI Workflow Engine parses natural language — type 'Onboard John as Software Engineer' or 'Promote Sarah to Senior Lead' — and executes it step by step. Employees can apply for leave; managers approve with one click. Performance reviews track quarterly ratings with AI-generated career suggestions. Promotions log position changes and salary revisions."

---

## 4. Training, Payroll & Notifications (3:00 – 3:45)

**Visual:** Training page → Payroll page → Notification dropdown → Dark mode toggle

**Voiceover:**
"Training tracks course progress and certificates. Payroll shows monthly salary breakdowns — base pay, deductions, bonuses, net pay. The notification bell pulls real-time alerts; unread items are highlighted. Dark mode toggles the entire UI, and the layout is fully responsive on mobile and tablet."

---

## 5. Closing (3:45 – 4:00)

**Visual:** App logo fade out

**Voiceover:**
"AI HR Employee combines modern UI with AI automation to make HR seamless. Check out the live demo or code on GitHub. Thanks for watching!"
